Our Story

From Landskrona
to the World

Born on the streets and pitches of Landskrona in 2024, Force Per4mance started as a youth development program for local kids chasing a dream. It quickly became the premier training destination for professional players in the region.

Now we're taking on our biggest challenge yet — creating an international pathway for talented players from around the globe to come to Scandinavia, get coached by elite staff, get seen by scouts, and carve out a professional football career.

🦁

Elite Coaching Staff

Our coaches have direct connections to professional clubs and scouting networks across Scandinavia and Europe.

🌍

International Reach

Players from across Africa, the Americas, Asia, and Europe have come through our doors. We're building a global football family in Landskrona.

🏆

Ambassadors of Landskrona

We don't just develop players — we bring the world to Landskrona, elevating the city through monthly international tournaments and events.
